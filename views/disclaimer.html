About/Usage...

CheckList Application (CLAPP) is used to work a checklist for flight simulation purposes only.

The checklists here are used specifically for flight simulation, and are adapted and not 100% accurate.

The Cold Start and the Closeout checklists are meant for my machine, so they are not intended to be 100% accurate.

If you would like to add a checklist, feel free to send it to me at dev at tiberiumsoftware dot com.
